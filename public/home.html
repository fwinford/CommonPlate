How It Works

For Requesters

  1. Fill out the request form with vendor, food, pickup name, time window, and email
  2. Receive confirmation email
  3. Another student places your order on GrubHub using their meal swipes
  4. Receive email with pickup details
  5. Pick up your food!

For Givers

  1. Browse active meal requests on the homepage
  2. Click a request to see full details
  3. Place the order using your meal swipes on GrubHub
  4. Enter the order number and estimated pickup time
  5. Requester automatically receives the order details
